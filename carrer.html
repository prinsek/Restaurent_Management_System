STAGIAIRE PROGRAMME

Here at the Prinse we have a long-standing programme welcoming chefs into our restaurant

on a stagiaire basis in order to learn, expand their knowledge and experience

the buzz of the Prinse kitchen.

we and the team are keen to share their kitchen and wealth of knowledge

with young chefs.

We can offer placements for 2 or 4 weeks and can be booked up to 4 months in advance.

Our stagiaire calendar is currently open until 31st December 2020.

Please email thekitchen@theprinse.com with a copy of your Curriculum Vitae, a little bit about

yourself and what you would like to gain from any time spent here.

Please note that the stagiaire placement will be unpaid and the Prinse does not

provide accommodation.

We only accept applicants with professional kitchen experience.
